Allocatively Efficiency

Allocatively Efficiency means that it's being produced where marginal benefit equals marginal cost.

We are also going to talk about Marginal social benefit/cost throughout this unit.


Marginal Social Benefit

is the benefit of a product to the entire economy.

Marginal Social Cost

is the cost of a product to the entire economy.

The true allocatively efficient outcome is where Marginal Social Benefit equals Marginal Social Cost.
